NADEZHDA SERGEEVA

Nadia Sergeeva's dream of Olympic glory turned into a nightmare at the 2018 PyeongChang Winter Games. The Russian bobsleigh racer, primed to compete alongside her teammate, tested positive for a banned substance - trimetazidine, a heart medication.

Hope turned to heartbreak as Nadia's results were disqualified. The positive test cast a shadow over not just her own participation but also Russia's already strained reputation due to past doping scandals. While Nadia claimed she never intentionally took the substance, the anti-doping violation stood.

Facing the consequences, Nadia accepted an eight-month ban. The once promising finish at the Olympics became a cautionary tale, highlighting the strict regulations and harsh penalties surrounding anti-doping.
